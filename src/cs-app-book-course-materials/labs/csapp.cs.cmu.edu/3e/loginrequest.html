Request a CS:APP Instructor Account

To request your CS:APP instructor's account, please fill out the enclosed form. We'll review it manually and get back to you in a day or so with a userid and password. Your account is good for any edition of the CS:APP text.

Please observe the following rules when you fill out the form so that we can authenticate your request. Requests that ignore these rules will be ignored.

  • Rule 1: The "Email" address must be from your school (bovik@cs.wm.edu), not from an ISP or free email service (e.g., bovik@verizon.net, bovik@gmail.com, or bovik@163.com).

  • Rule 2: The "Official Web page" must be an official English language Web page from your institution that lists you as a faculty or instructor and that gives your school email address. (Please don't just give us the main home page of your institution!)
